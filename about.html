Why we exist

The Problem

100,000 startups die every year. Not because they built bad products. Because nobody knew they existed.

Worse products with better stories raised more money, hired better people, and won.

What We Believe

Tech is as much culture as it is product.

If you can't be in the news, you can't build in tech. If you can't differentiate, you disappear. And differentiation doesn't come from features — it comes from story, taste, and culture.

Every company is now a media company. The ones who understand this win. The ones who don't become case studies in what not to do.

Products die every day. Great stories live forever.

Who We Are

Explicit is a creative studio at the intersection of art and technology.

We've spent time on both sides — building products and building brands. We understand metrics and media. Product and positioning. CAC and creativity.

We've worked with pre-seed founders and companies doing $100M+ ARR. We know what narratives move markets, what stories raise rounds, and what content actually drives growth.

  • We don't do campaigns. We build culture.
  • We don't chase trends. We start movements.
  • We don't make things that look good. We make things that work.

And we tie everything back to business outcomes. Because creative work that doesn't solve real problems is just decoration.

Who We're For

Technical founders who know marketing matters but have no idea where to start.

VCs who understand that brand is now a competitive advantage — and want deal flow that comes to them.

Companies who refuse to look like everyone else. Who want to be known, not just seen.

If you're building something that matters and struggling to make people give a shit, we should talk.
