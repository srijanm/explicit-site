Why we exist

100,000 startups die every year. Most of them had great products. They died because no one knew they existed.

The internet is littered with the carcasses of startups who couldn't get people to care. Not because they weren't building something valuable. But because they never learned to tell their story.

Products die every day. Great stories live forever.

If you don't tell your story, someone else will. And they won't tell it right.

In a world drowning in noise, the companies that win aren't always the best. They're the ones people remember.

Who we are

Explicit is a creative studio at the intersection of art and technology.

We've spent time on both ends — building products and building brands. We understand metrics and media, product and positioning, CAC and creativity.

We don't do campaigns. We build culture. We start movements. We make sure people give a shit.

Who we're for

  • Technical founders who know marketing is important but feels like a black hole.
  • VCs who understand they need to stand out to win the best deals.
  • Companies who refuse to be ignored.
