Touching Grass

Owned the moment for "touching grass" by creating a product that was literally, grass.

Role Co-founder, Brand Experience Design
Year 2024

The Goal

Own the moment around the growing "touching grass" movement in tech circles which talks about taking a break from screen time & being chronically online.

The How

Athletic Greens (AG1) is a very popular supplement being promoted heavily but was seen suspiciously by health folks.

The Why

We created a parody of AG1 called TG2 (Touching Grass 2) which caught attention by being exactly like AG1 but was a patch of astroturf as a reminder to go outside.

Impact

Ownership of the popular cultural trope of "touching grass". Months later, people still tag us on anything to do with touching grass.

500 touching grass packets ordered
100k+ views across channels
$0 ad or influencer spend
∞ cultural impact
