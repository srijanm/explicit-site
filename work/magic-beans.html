Magic Beans & Insider

Launched India's top health & wellness community with 400 of the country's most influential people in the field.

Role Co-founder, Brand Experience Design, Community Design
Year 2024

The Goal

Build the go-to community for biohackers, longevity nerds & top tier health folks in the country.

The How

Supplements are extremely popular and seen by many as replacements for living a healthy life. Everyone wants a magic pill that will fix their health problems without going to the gym, eating right or taking care of themselves.

The Why

We realised most of the longevity folks lived on Twitter so we made the Magic Beans bottles & launched on Twitter. Ended up going viral and found the best folks for the community. The community is still very much active.

Impact

In under 24 hours

1000+ signups
> 100k views
$0 ad or influencer spend
100+ tech twitter mentions
