Pause

Built one of the most loved mental health brands by focussing on relatability & memes instead of mollycoddling. Lowered CAC, commanded higher prices & had some of the best word of mouth growth in a very tough business.

The Goal

To build a mental health brand that stood out from the molly coddling approach taken by all other mental health brands.

The How

Go against the grain & make mental health more relatable by laughing about it.

The What

Became a meme page, choosing to entertain rather than educate. Never promoted our therapy services & ended up getting more users because of that.

Impact

Revolutionized mental health marketing and increased therapy adoption

CAC lowest in category
10k organic followers
60 NPS
>100 meme page features
